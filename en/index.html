Server Rules

No Cheating or Griefing

Includes but not limited to hacks, griefing other players' buildings, political discussions, insults, etc.

About Zhm Bucket

Focused on relaxed socializing and free survival, redstone, building, and being a hard worker.

  • Stable online status and good maintenance
  • Friendly community and helpful atmosphere

/tpa <player ID>Request to teleport to player
/tpacceptAccept teleport request
/tpahere <player ID> <player>Request player to teleport here
/tpadenyDeny teleport request
